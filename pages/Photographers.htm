Photographers and Their Work

With the understanding that the work of any given photographer can evolve through a range of different aesthetics during his or her career, the following artists have been selected to represent the distinction between formalism and imitationalism.

Formalism

Imitationalism
Images in which the predominant concern is the formal structure of the composition. Formalist images tend towards design for its own sake. Images in which the predominant concern is the communication of an idea or concern. Imitationalist images tend to be documentary in nature.
